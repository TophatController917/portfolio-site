Hello and Welcome!

I'm Beckett, a programmer with a passion for creating fun and interesting games, and a student at Michigan State University.

Below are a few projects I've worked on; you can click into them to see more information or play them yourself. Up above, you can see the rest of my work and more about me!

Knight Fight

A top-down fighting game where players do battle as one of four unique knights in intense skill-based combat

I developed Knight Fight in a group of two over the course of 5 weeks in Winter of 2024, serving as the project's lead gameplay and systems programmer, and collaborating as a designer.

Gordon Grappler

A cooking platformer where the player uses a grappling hook to navigate a kitchen, grab ingredients and cook meals. Gordon Grappler was voted Audience Choice and Best Soundtrack of MSU’s 48-hour Fall 2023 Game Jam.

While working on the project, I served as one of two programmers and helped design the core gameplay mechanics.

Goose Genie

A physics-based platformer where the player must switch between 3 states of matter to navigate the environment and solve puzzles.

I made Goose Geneie alongside a team of over 70 as a part of MSU's Spartasoft Studio from January to May of 2024.
